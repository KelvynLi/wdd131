The Jaguar: Majesty of the Pantanal

The jaguar (Panthera onca) is the largest feline in the Americas and a symbol of the Pantanal's rich biodiversity.

Agricultural production

Mato Grosso has been consolidating itself as one of the largest grain producers in Brazil, with a significant share of national production.

Tourist Attractions

  • Pantanal: The world's largest tropical wetland, known for its incredible biodiversity and wildlife.
  • Cuiabá: The capital city, offering cultural experiences, local cuisine, and historical sites.
  • Chapada dos Guimarães: A national park with stunning landscapes, waterfalls, and hiking trails.
