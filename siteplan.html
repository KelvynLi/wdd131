Site Planning Document – Project W05

Site Name

Drive Eco is a dealership specializing in electric and hybrid vehicles in Brazil. The name conveys sustainability, technology, and smart mobility.

Optional Domain: driveverde.com.br

Site Purpose

This website will serve as an informational and commercial hub for Drive Eco, offering:

  • An updated catalog of available vehicles
  • Financing services and payment options
  • Test drive scheduling and customer support
  • Educational content about electric mobility

Scenarios

  • What models of electric cars are currently available for immediate delivery?
  • What tax incentives are offered for customers purchasing hybrid vehicles?

Color Scheme

  • Green (#0B9444): used for titles, icons, and buttons
  • White (#FFFFFF): background color for clean contrast and readability
  • Dark Gray (#2D2D2D): used for body text, footer, and headers

Typography

  • Montserrat: used for titles and navigation menus
  • Roboto: used for paragraph text and body content
  • Oswald (optional): used for slogans or highlighted sections
